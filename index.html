Diversity Daily strives to bring back the balance of natural environments and combat the decline of biodiversity in the world.

How to Start:
Step 1: Understanding

Reading the Biodiversity Daily articles will inform you on global issues, the types of plants the seeds will produce, and which animals you are affecting in the ecosystem."

Step 2: Action

After reading the articles, tear up the newspaper and plant the ripped paper in a flower bed, pot, or just in your lawn. Water them occassionally.

Impact:

By planting our newspaper, you are increasing the natural flora which attracts the return/ abundance of native fauna. You're also returning the soil to a healthy composition.
